📚 Chapter-wise Topics for Today
⚡ Physics – Moving Charges & Magnetism (~4 marks)
MUST
Biot-Savart Law & Applications
B at centre of circular loop. Direct numerical every year.
MUST
Ampere's Circuital Law
B inside solenoid = μ₀nI. Toroid formula.
MUST
Force on Moving Charge in B field
F = qvB sinθ. Radius r = mv/qB. Most repeated formula.
MUST
Force on Current-carrying Conductor
F = BIL sinθ. Force between parallel wires.
IMP
Cyclotron – Principle & Frequency
Cyclotron frequency = qB/2πm; independent of velocity.
IMP
Moving Coil Galvanometer
Deflection ∝ current. Current sensitivity = NAB/k.
IMP
Torque on Current Loop
τ = NBIA sinθ. Magnetic dipole moment m = NIA.
MED
Long Straight Wire Field
B = μ₀I/2πr. Know direction via right-hand rule.
🧪 Chemistry – d & f Block Elements (~4-5 marks)
MUST
Electronic Configuration of d-block
Exceptions: Cr [Ar]3d⁵4s¹ and Cu [Ar]3d¹⁰4s¹. 1-2 MCQs.
MUST
Variable Oxidation States
Mn: +2 to +7. Reason: (n-1)d and ns electrons available.
MUST
Magnetic Properties
μ = √n(n+2) BM. Count unpaired electrons. Paramagnetism.
MUST
KMnO₄ – Preparation, Properties, Uses
MnO₄⁻ is strong oxidising agent. Reactions in acidic/basic/neutral media.
MUST
K₂Cr₂O₇ – Preparation & Uses
Dichromate in acidic medium: Cr₂O₇²⁻ + 14H⁺ + 6e⁻ → 2Cr³⁺
IMP
Lanthanoid Contraction – Cause & Effects
Imperfect shielding by 4f electrons → atomic radii decrease.
IMP
Colour in d-block Compounds
d-d transition. Zn²⁺, Cu⁺, Sc³⁺ are colourless (no unpaired e⁻).
MED
Actinoids vs Lanthanoids Comparison
Irregular oxidation states in actinoids. 5f vs 4f shielding.
📐 Maths – Continuity & Differentiability (~5-6 marks)
MUST
Continuity at a Point
LHL = RHL = f(a). Finding k for continuity. Direct MCQ.
MUST
Differentiability & Relation to Continuity
Every differentiable function is continuous. Converse false.
MUST
Chain Rule (Composite Functions)
d/dx[f(g(x))] = f'(g(x))·g'(x). Most questions use this.
MUST
Logarithmic Differentiation
y = xˢⁱⁿˣ or xˣ type. Take log both sides before diff.
MUST
Implicit Differentiation
Differentiate both sides w.r.t. x; group dy/dx terms.
IMP
Parametric Differentiation
dy/dx = (dy/dt)/(dx/dt). Common with x=acosθ, y=asinθ.
IMP
Second Order Derivatives d²y/dx²
Differentiate dy/dx again. Used in Rolle's theorem problems.
IMP
Rolle's Theorem & LMVT
Rolle's: f(a)=f(b) → f'(c)=0. LMVT: f'(c) = [f(b)-f(a)]/(b-a).
💡 KCET Cheat Codes & Exam Strategy – Day 3
PHY
Right-Hand Thumb Rule: Thumb → current direction; fingers curl in direction of B field around the wire.
PHY
Cyclotron radius: r = mv/qB. Doubling speed → doubles radius. Doubling mass → doubles radius.
PHY
Parallel wires trick: Same direction currents attract; opposite direction currents repel.
CHEM
Cr & Cu exception: Half-filled (Cr) and fully-filled (Cu) d-orbitals are extra stable → 4s gets only 1e⁻.
CHEM
Colour quick check: d⁰ (Sc³⁺, Ti⁴⁺) and d¹⁰ (Zn²⁺, Cu⁺) are colourless. All others coloured.
CHEM
KMnO₄ colour change: Acidic → Mn⁷⁺(purple) reduces to Mn²⁺(colourless/pale pink). Easy identification MCQ.
MATH
Continuity trick: If function has piecewise definition, always check at the breaking point. Substitute from both sides.
MATH
Log diff shortcut: For y = [f(x)]^g(x), take ln, differentiate: (1/y)(dy/dx) = g'lnf + g·f'/f.
MATH
|x| not differentiable at x=0: LHD = -1, RHD = +1. Not equal → not differentiable. Common trap question.
🧮 Formula Sheet – Day 3 Chapters
⚡ Moving Charges & Magnetism – Physics
BIOT-SAVART LAW
dB = μ₀/4π · (I dl × r̂)/r²
μ₀ = 4π × 10⁻⁷ T·m/A
FIELD AT CENTRE OF CIRCULAR LOOP
B = μ₀I / 2R
R = radius of loop; for N turns: B = μ₀NI/2R
LONG STRAIGHT WIRE
B = μ₀I / 2πr
r = perpendicular distance from wire
SOLENOID (AMPERE'S LAW)
B = μ₀nI
n = turns per unit length = N/L
FORCE ON MOVING CHARGE
F = q(v × B) = qvB sinθ
Circular motion if v ⊥ B
RADIUS OF CIRCULAR PATH
r = mv / qB
Time period T = 2πm/qB (independent of v)
CYCLOTRON FREQUENCY
ν = qB / 2πm
Independent of velocity and radius
FORCE ON CURRENT CONDUCTOR
F = BIL sinθ
θ = angle between I and B
FORCE PER UNIT LENGTH (PARALLEL WIRES)
F/L = μ₀I₁I₂ / 2πd
Attractive if currents in same direction
TORQUE ON CURRENT LOOP
τ = NBIA sinθ | τ = m × B
m = NIA = magnetic dipole moment
GALVANOMETER SENSITIVITY
I/θ = k/NAB
Current sensitivity = NAB/k; voltage sensitivity = NAB/kR
INTERNAL RESISTANCE (POTENTIOMETER)
r = R(l₁ - l₂)/l₂
l₁ = without shunt; l₂ = with shunt R
🧪 d & f Block Elements – Chemistry
MAGNETIC MOMENT
μ = √[n(n+2)] BM
n = number of unpaired electrons
DICHROMATE HALF-REACTION (ACIDIC)
Cr₂O₇²⁻ + 14H⁺ + 6e⁻ → 2Cr³⁺ + 7H₂O
Strong oxidising agent in acidic medium
PERMANGANATE (ACIDIC MEDIUM)
MnO₄⁻ + 8H⁺ + 5e⁻ → Mn²⁺ + 4H₂O
Purple → colourless/pale pink
PERMANGANATE (BASIC MEDIUM)
MnO₄⁻ + e⁻ → MnO₄²⁻
Purple → green (manganate)
Cr ELECTRONIC CONFIGURATION
[Ar] 3d⁵ 4s¹
NOT 3d⁴4s². Half-filled 3d⁵ extra stable
Cu ELECTRONIC CONFIGURATION
[Ar] 3d¹⁰ 4s¹
NOT 3d⁹4s². Fully filled 3d¹⁰ extra stable
LANTHANOIDS
Ce (Z=58) to Lu (Z=71) | 4f¹⁻¹⁴
Lanthanoid contraction: imperfect shielding by 4f
ACTINOIDS
Th (Z=90) to Lr (Z=103) | 5f¹⁻¹⁴
All radioactive; wider range of oxidation states vs lanthanoids
📐 Continuity & Differentiability – Mathematics
CONTINUITY AT POINT a
lim[x→a⁻]f(x) = lim[x→a⁺]f(x) = f(a)
LHL = RHL = f(a) must all be equal
DIFFERENTIABILITY (LHD = RHD)
f'(a) = lim[h→0] [f(a+h)-f(a)]/h
Differentiable → continuous; not vice versa
CHAIN RULE
d/dx[f(g(x))] = f'(g(x))·g'(x)
Apply from outside in
LOGARITHMIC DIFFERENTIATION
y = f(x)^g(x) → ln y = g ln f → diff both sides
Use for variable base and exponent
IMPLICIT DIFFERENTIATION
F(x,y) = 0 → dy/dx = -∂F/∂x ÷ ∂F/∂y
Or differentiate term by term w.r.t. x
PARAMETRIC FORM
dy/dx = (dy/dt) / (dx/dt)
d²y/dx² = (d/dt[dy/dx]) / (dx/dt)
ROLLE'S THEOREM
f continuous on [a,b], f(a)=f(b) → ∃c: f'(c)=0
c lies strictly between a and b
LMVT (MEAN VALUE THEOREM)
f'(c) = [f(b) - f(a)] / (b - a)
Geometrically: tangent || secant at c
d/dx (sin⁻¹x)
1 / √(1-x²)
Domain: (-1,1)
d/dx (cos⁻¹x)
-1 / √(1-x²)
Note the negative sign!
d/dx (tan⁻¹x)
1 / (1+x²)
Domain: all real numbers
d/dx (xⁿ) | d/dx (eˣ) | d/dx (ln x)
nxⁿ⁻¹ | eˣ | 1/x
Memorise these; they appear in every question
🎯 Quick Cheat Codes — Chapter Endings (Score 45+)
⚡ Physics Tricks
Helix motion: When v has component || B → helical path; pitch = v‖ × T.

Velocity selector: qE = qvB → v = E/B. No deflection.

Ammeter vs Voltmeter: Ammeter = low resistance (shunt in parallel). Voltmeter = high resistance (multiplier in series).
🧪 Chemistry Tricks
Mn oxidation states: +2 (most stable in solution), +4 (MnO₂), +7 (KMnO₄).

Highest oxidation state: Equal to group number. Os, Ru can show +8.

Lanthanoid ions coloured: Due to f-f transition (not d-d like transition metals).
📐 Maths Tricks
Even/odd check: f(-x)=f(x) → even; f(-x)=-f(x) → odd. Helps simplify diff.

LMVT existence: Need f continuous on [a,b] AND differentiable on (a,b).

y = xˣ derivative: Use log diff → dy/dx = xˣ(1 + ln x). Classic KCET question.
